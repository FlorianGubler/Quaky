Quaky

"Quaky" is a useful app to see all earthquakes that currently happen and happend in the last years. You can search specificly by date and see all measured activities. Also you become direct notifications if a new one happend. For direct accessability a widget with the last earthquake happend, is also available. For those one who are interested, many details for the earthquakes are available and you can also go to the official website, that published the choosen earthquake.
